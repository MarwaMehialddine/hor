capricorn ZODIAC SIGN: CHARACTERISTICS, DATES, & MORE


capricorn overview


symbol: sea-goat

date range: dec 22 - jan 19

element: earth

modality: cardinal

ruling planet: saturn

compatibility: capricorn - virgo

house: ten

tarot card: The devil

color: brown - grey

Capricorn (♑︎) Latin for "horned goat", is the tenth astrological sign in the zodiac out of twelve total zodiac signs, originating from the constellation of Capricornus, the goat. It spans the 270–300th degree of the zodiac, corresponding to celestial longitude. Under the tropical zodiac, the sun transits this area from about December 22 to January 19. In astrology, Capricorn is considered an earth sign, negative sign, and one of the four cardinal signs. Capricorn is said to be ruled by the planet Saturn.


capricorn in friendships


Capricorn takes friendships incredibly seriously.


The Capricorn friendship is a delicate matter. Obviously Capricorn trusts only few friends. Therefore Capricorn isn’t too interested in having many friends, the fewer, the better. Capricorn believes that having a few but good friends proves better than having too many. He would get to know them better with time to come.


Besides, Capricorns considers friends part of their family. This is a normal thing to do for Capricorns based on their trust for his relatives. To secure his friend’s trust, it means that Capricorns will try to share more with them expect their private life which never shared with anyone.


Capricorns make great friends! You are loyal, funny, and dependable, and while Goats may not be the best communicators, your actions speak louder than words. You're the best at planning fun nights for your small (but super close) group of friends thanks to how practical and organized you are. While you can be stubborn and pessimistic, you are always there for your besties.



capricorn in love & romance


TCapricorns are natural bosses and carry a noticeable air of authority, even in their loving relationships. This could be anything from a little domineering to an all-out control freak, but either way, they may have a tendency to treat you as their support team rather than their equal partner. Capricorns can also be very opportunistic, which may end up with you feeling like you're being used.If there's anyone who can find the bad in a situation, it's a Capricorn. Their tendency to look at the gloomy side of life could wear on you quickly if you carry a more upbeat, positive outlook on things.


Capricorns are serious monogamists, and although they can be cautious in every other aspect of their lives, they are the sign who is likely to fall deeply in love with someone, and it’s not unusual for a Capricorn to get married after only several months of dating. They know their hearts well, and don’t hesitate to lock things down when they know that the relationship is right. And their instincts are on point—loyal Capricorns stay in relationships for the long haul. They aren’t afraid of therapy if their relationship needs help, and approach problems as a bump in the road, capable of fixing with honesty, trust, and hard work from both partners.


Capricorn's demeanor is classy and respectful -- while they may not be the life of the party, they won't ever embarrass you with loud, rude, or obnoxious behavior either. When you show them off to your friends or family, you can relax knowing your Capricorn lover will make a good impression.
